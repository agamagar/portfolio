The questionnaires helped us understand the core painpoints of the printing experience.

Bulk Printing

Why printing in bulk works best

Inventory Planning

Bulk prining helped vendors predict and plan the raw materials they needed to get every month. Further helping them optimise their business.

Cheaper

Bulk printing is much cheaper compared to individual printing for students, bringing down the prices was the major factor dolding back students.

Less Errors

Bulk printing is more presice compared to regular printng due to the mechanism of the machines. Eg- T-shirt heat presses are much more effecient when heated up, which takes time leading the regular orders to be messed up.
